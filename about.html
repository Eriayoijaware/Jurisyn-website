About Jurisyn

Helping African tech thrive globally through smart compliance.

Bridging Law and Innovation

I'm Busola Ijaware, the founder of Jurisyn. As a law student specializing in Data Protection and Innovation Law, I’m deeply embedded in the evolving legal landscape that governs how tech companies operate across borders.

Jurisyn was born from a simple observation: African startups are building world-class tech but are often slowed down by the high cost of international compliance. I’ve focused my research and work on making GDPR and NDPR standards accessible to early-stage founders.
